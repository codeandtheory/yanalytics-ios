AnalyticsEventFactory

public protocol AnalyticsEventFactory

Anything that generates an analytics event.

Three protocols, ScreenViewFactory, UserPropertyFactory, and EventFactory, conform to AnalyticsEventFactory; one for each of the three cases in the AnalyticsEvent enum.
